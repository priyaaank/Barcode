Group chat between distributed teams

Be it real time or just a dumping ground for whatever happens in your day. It's there, to be read and used by the part of team on the other side of the world. It's the real distributed chat.

File transcripts and history

Pipe maintains your chat history. You can browse and read transcripts by date; mark favorites and search through the rubble. All in a snap!
